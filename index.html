About Boza

Boza is a fermented beverage with a thick, malt-like drink made from fermented grains, typically corn, wheat, or millet, and is popular in Central Asia, the Balkans, Turkey, and other regions. It has a subtle tart, tangy taste and a very low alcohol content.
